Click the circles as fast and as accurately as you can before the timer runs out.

  • Left click to score. Missing breaks your combo.
  • Targets shrink with difficulty. Smaller = more points.
  • Escaped targets don’t affect accuracy but will hurt your final score.
  • Press Space or the Pause button to pause/resume.
